Project Name

EduAgent AI

Product Vision

EduAgent AI is an adaptive AI learning platform that teaches students in two ways:

from uploaded academic materials
from general subject/topic requests without uploads

It helps students understand topics deeply, revise faster, and prepare for exams through tutoring, summaries, voice explanations, quizzes, scheduling, and progress tracking.

Problem Statement

Students often:

do not understand lectures in real time,
need help revising after class,
want a tutor even when they do not have notes,
struggle to break down difficult topics,
need personalized learning, not generic chat answers.

EduAgent AI solves this by acting as a smart tutor that can teach from documents or teach from pure topic requests.

Functional Requirements
FR1: Authentication

Users can sign up, log in, and manage profiles.

FR2: Upload Study Material

Users can upload academic files for analysis.

FR3: Topic Entry Without Uploads

Users can type a topic and get a full tutoring session without uploading anything.

FR4: AI Lesson Generation

The system must generate structured lessons from either documents or topics.

FR5: Semantic Retrieval

The system must retrieve relevant document chunks when materials are uploaded.

FR6: Voice Tutoring

The system must support spoken explanations.

FR7: Notes Generation

The system must automatically generate revision notes.

FR8: Scheduling

The system must allow recurring lesson scheduling.

FR9: Progress Tracking

The system must track learning performance and weak areas.

FR10: Quiz Generation

The system must generate practice questions and answers.
